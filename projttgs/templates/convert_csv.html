How to use voice commands

Single row fill:

  • "ID is T001" — fills Teacher ID in the next empty row
  • "name is Dr Amit Sharma" — fills the Name field
  • "designation is Professor" — fills Designation
  • "workload is 25" — fills Max Workload

Fill full row at once:

  • "T001 comma Dr Amit comma Professor comma 25" — fills all columns in order

Batch fill (multiple rows):

  • "IDs are T001, T002, T003" — fills T001, T002, T003 in 3 separate rows
  • "names are Amit, Priya, Rahul" — fills Name in 3 rows

Navigation:

  • "next row" — move to the next row
  • "stop" — stop voice input

Keep speaking — voice stays active until you click Stop or say "stop".
