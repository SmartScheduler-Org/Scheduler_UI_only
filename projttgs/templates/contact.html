Everything you need to know about SmartScheduler.

SmartScheduler's genetic algorithm engine generates a fully optimized, clash-free timetable in 3–8 seconds for most institutions. The engine evaluates thousands of schedule combinations across all sections, rooms, faculty, and meeting-time slots simultaneously — so what used to take a coordinator weeks is done before you finish your coffee.
Yes — labs are first-class citizens in SmartScheduler. Lab sessions span 2 or more consecutive slots, are scheduled in shared lab rooms with the correct instructor, and are grouped into logically continuous blocks. Open electives and multi-discipline courses (aligned with NEP 2020's flexible credit structure) are also supported, with separate elective batch allocation per section.
Yes. Every generated timetable can be exported as a PDF in one click — both section-wise and teacher-wise views are supported. The export engine uses WeasyPrint and xhtml2pdf under the hood to produce print-ready documents. Saved timetables can also be re-downloaded at any time from your dashboard.
SmartScheduler supports API-based ERP syncing to keep courses, rooms, and faculty data always up to date. You can also bulk-import instructor–course mappings via CSV upload — just drop a file with instructor_name, course_number columns and the system maps them automatically. This removes the need to re-enter data each semester.
Yes. The Teacher Preference module lets HODs send personalized preference-form links to each teacher via email (SMTP). Teachers open their link — no login required — and submit their preferred time slots, days off, and course preferences. The HOD can view all submitted responses on a single dashboard, export them as CSV, and the engine uses these preferences as soft constraints during generation.
SmartScheduler has three distinct roles:

HOD / Admin — Full access: manage departments, rooms, instructors, courses, sections, generate timetables, configure preferences, and manage subscriptions.
Teacher — View their personal timetable and submit availability preferences.
Dean — Read-only overview of all department timetables for institutional review.

Role assignment happens at login. Each user is directed to the appropriate dashboard automatically.
Yes — Pro plan users get drag-and-drop editing to move any class to a different slot directly on the timetable grid. There's also a substitute scheduling feature that lets you quickly reassign a class to a different instructor for a session — useful for sudden absences — without regenerating the entire timetable.
SmartScheduler offers two plans:

Basic — A limited number of timetable generation credits per month, suitable for small departments or trial use. No drag-and-drop or substitute scheduling.
Pro — Unlimited generation credits, full drag-and-drop editing, substitute scheduling, and priority support.

Payments are processed securely via Razorpay. You can upgrade at any time from the subscription page.
