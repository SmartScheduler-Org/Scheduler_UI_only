Privacy Policy

Your data, handled with care.

SmartScheduler is built to simplify academic scheduling while respecting the information institutions, administrators, and teachers share with us. This page explains what we collect, why we collect it, and how we protect it when you use the platform.

Last updated: April 17, 2026
What we collect
Account details, scheduling inputs, support messages, and limited technical data needed to keep SmartScheduler working reliably.
How we protect it
We use access controls, authenticated sessions, and operational safeguards to reduce unauthorized access and keep institutional data secure.
Your control
You can contact us to review, update, or request deletion of personal information wherever applicable to your institution and account.
Quick guide
How SmartScheduler treats privacy

We only ask for the data needed to run the scheduling platform, support users, and improve reliability. We do not design the product around unnecessary collection.

1. Information we collect

When you use SmartScheduler, we may collect information that you or your institution enters into the platform in order to generate and manage academic timetables.

  • Account and profile details such as username, email address, and role within the institution.
  • Scheduling data such as departments, courses, sections, teachers, rooms, meeting times, and timetable preferences.
  • Messages sent through our contact form, including your name, email, institution name, topic, and message content.
  • Basic technical information required for site operation, authentication, and performance monitoring.
2. How we use information

We use collected information to operate SmartScheduler and deliver the features users expect from the product.

  • Generate, display, edit, save, and export timetables.
  • Support role-based access for admins, teachers, and deans.
  • Respond to product questions, support requests, and contact submissions.
  • Maintain platform security, prevent misuse, and improve scheduling reliability.
We do not collect personal information for unrelated advertising purposes on this website.
3. Sharing and disclosure

We do not sell personal information. Information may be shared only where needed to operate the service or comply with legal obligations.

  • With service providers that support hosting, payments, email delivery, or core platform operations.
  • Within your institution, based on the role and permissions granted to your account.
  • When required by law, regulation, court order, or to protect the rights, safety, and security of the platform.
4. Security and retention

We take reasonable steps to protect the information stored in SmartScheduler. No online service can guarantee absolute security, but we work to reduce risk through technical and administrative safeguards.

  • Authenticated access is used for protected workflows and role-specific dashboards.
  • Data is retained for as long as needed to provide the service, maintain records, and support institutional use.
  • Support communications may be retained for follow-up, troubleshooting, and service quality purposes.
5. Your choices

You may request updates or corrections to personal information connected to your account. Where applicable, you may also request deletion, subject to operational, institutional, or legal requirements.

If your institution manages your SmartScheduler account, some requests may need to be coordinated through your authorized administrator.

6. Contact us about privacy

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or want to make a privacy-related request, you can reach out to us directly.
